// About the Fest

Where Intelligence
Meets Innovation

AI-nstein 2K26 is the annual technical and cultural symposium of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Noorul Islam Centre for Higher Education, Kanyakumari. A collision of intellect, creativity, and competition — it brings together students from across institutions to challenge each other through high-stakes technical events, electrifying cultural performances, and everything in between. Named after the greatest mind in history and the intelligence redefining the future, AI-nstein 2K26 is more than a fest — it is a statement that the next generation of problem-solvers, builders, and innovators is already here.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at NICHE has been shaping technology professionals since 1989 — one of the institution's two founding departments. With a vision rooted in quality academic and research-oriented education, the department equips students with both the technical depth and problem-solving mindset needed to thrive in a fast-evolving global industry.

Backed by an Intel® Unnati Server-powered lab and a faculty spanning AI, IoT, Robotics, Cloud Computing, and Data Science, the department bridges classroom learning with real-world application — consistently pushing the boundaries of what students can build, research, and achieve.
